IEEE PES CHAPTER INAUGRATION

01/09/2014
Venue:EED PG seminar hall

The formal inauguration of IEEE PES Student Chapter NIT Calicut was held at EED PG seminar hall at 11:15 AM on 01/09/2014. The welcome speech was given by Dr. Ashok S. Professor, Department of Electrical Engineering NIT Calicut. Thereafter Chief guest Sri. M. Muhammad Ali Rawther, Director (Generation & Transmission- Electrical), KSEB, Kerala inaugurated the IEEE PES Chapter in NITC Campus and also delivered an expert lecture on “Smart Grids

Initiatives in Kerala- Challenges”. The chief guest was honored by Dr. Jeevamma Jacob, Professor and Head, Department of Electrical Engineering. At the end of the function, Vote of Thanks was given by Dr. Kumaravel S., Assistant Professor, Department of Electrical Engineering. The program was organized in association with IEEE PES Kerala Section.

LINUX WORKSHOP II

Date: 30-8-2014
Total Participants : 40

The second edition of the Linux workshop was organized at AWH College of Engineering, Kuttikattoor and KMCT College of Engineering, Calicut. There were 40 participants in both the colleges. LINUX WORKSHOP I

Date: 23-8-2014
Total Participants : 16

The WIE chapter of IEEE NITC Student Branch in association with WIE Malabar Subsection organized an introductory LINUX workshop at MEA College of Engineering, Perinthalmanna. 16 students participated in the workshop. The participants were Btech first year and second year students.

A team of 9 WIE members had gone and classes were handled by Ms. Soumya, Ms. Vishnupriya and Ms. Ragja P. The workshop was held on 23rd August 2014 at the MEA, Perinthalmanna campus. Students were introduced to the world of LINUX and the endless possibilities of LINUX shell scripting.

PRE-TATHVA WORKSHOP

Date: 23-8-2014
Total Participants : 396

The Pre-Tathva MSP430 workshop is an initiative by tathva’14 in association with IEEE NITC Student Branch. This workshop is aimed at providing a deep technical knowledge to the students relating to the programming of microcontrollers and introduction to the state-of-the-art MSP430 series of Microcontrollers by Texas Instruments.

This workshop was structured for two full days and was held on 23rd and 24th of August 2014 within the NIT Calicut campus. The participants were students enrolled in various B-Tech programs of NIT Calicut.

INTO THE CIRCUIT

Date: 4-7-2014

This was the second edition of the Into The Circuit Wokshop. This workshop was aimed at providing practical insights to school students into the broad field of electronics. AVR WORKSHOP

Date: 5-4-2014
Total Participants:50

The AVR Microcontroller workshop was conducted as a two-day event at Embedded Systems Lab, ECED. This workshop introduced the participants to various concepts of Microcontroller Architecture and Programming. Various modules of microcontroller programming was implemented like GPIO, Timers, ADC’s, UART, Interfacing to LCD etc.

The participants were able to grab an in-depth knowledge of microcontroller programming and the workshop enabled them to implement various projects and ideas with the skills acquired.This workshop was an open event. This workshop was mainly attended by 4th and 6th semester B-Tech students from ECE and EEE departments.

TECHNOTRIX 2014

Date: 5-4-2014
Total Participants:52

The IEEE Technotrix was a 2 day basic introductory electronics workshop. It introduced various electronic concepts regarding transistors and other semiconductor devices.

The workshop covered simulation software –Proteus. This was to give the students an insight into the simulating and implementing electronic circuits. The participants were given in-depth training in designing and fabricating a custom Printed Circuit Board.
